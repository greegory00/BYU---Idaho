The White Water Rafting we offer is totally different from the rest.

We take all the advantages that white water gives us to offer you the best rafting experience, a great and exciting experience without risks. Our meeting point is in the hotel, where our changing rooms are. There, you’ll be equipped (you’ll be able to leave all your stuff safe until you’re back). From this point, we will take you to the ww rafting starting point just 6 minutes away. This transfer is just included for groups of 20 people or under.
